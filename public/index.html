Hello there 👋

I’m Alan Rudzinski, a self-taught web developer from Warsaw, Poland.

As an electrical engineer, I have discovered an interest in programming during my task about programming microcontrollers(using C language). Since then, I have deepen my interest focusing on web technologies, enhancing my coding skills by taking many courses and building a variety of projects - most of them are personal although I have some experience with commercial and collaborative tasks.
